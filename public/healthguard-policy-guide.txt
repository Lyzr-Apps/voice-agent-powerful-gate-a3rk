=== COVERAGE DETAILS ===

PREVENTIVE CARE (All Plans):
All HealthGuard plans cover preventive care at 100% with no copay or deductible. This includes:
- Annual wellness exam/physical
- Immunizations and vaccinations (flu shot, COVID-19, etc.)
- Cancer screenings (mammograms, colonoscopies, pap smears)
- Cholesterol and blood pressure screenings
- Diabetes screening
- Depression screening
- Well-child visits
- Prenatal care visits

HOSPITAL STAYS:
- Inpatient hospital stay: Covered at 80% after deductible (Basic), 85% after deductible (Plus), 90% after deductible (Premium)
- Semi-private room and board
- Operating and recovery room
- Intensive care
- Prescription drugs during stay
- Lab tests and X-rays during stay
- Pre-admission testing within 7 days

SURGERY:
- Inpatient surgery: Same as hospital stay coverage
- Outpatient surgery: $200 copay (Basic), $150 copay (Plus), $100 copay (Premium)
- Pre-authorization required for elective surgeries
- Second opinion covered at no additional cost

MATERNITY CARE:
- Prenatal visits: Covered as preventive care (100%)
- Hospital delivery: Covered as inpatient hospital stay
- 48-hour stay for vaginal delivery, 96 hours for C-section
- Newborn care covered from birth
- Breast pumps covered at 100%
- Lactation counseling: 6 visits covered

MENTAL HEALTH AND SUBSTANCE ABUSE:
- Outpatient therapy: Copay varies by plan
- Inpatient treatment: Covered same as medical/surgical inpatient
- No visit limits for outpatient mental health
- Substance abuse treatment covered at parity with medical care
- Crisis services covered 24/7

REHABILITATION SERVICES:
- Physical therapy: 40 visits per year
- Occupational therapy: 40 visits per year
- Speech therapy: 30 visits per year
- Cardiac rehabilitation: 36 sessions
- Pulmonary rehabilitation: 36 sessions
- Copay same as specialist visit

=== NETWORK PROVIDERS ===

IN-NETWORK vs OUT-OF-NETWORK:
- In-network providers have agreed to HealthGuard's negotiated rates
- Out-of-network providers may result in higher costs
- Balance billing protection applies for emergency services
- Out-of-network coverage: 60% of allowed amount after out-of-network deductible

HOW TO FIND A PROVIDER:
- Visit healthguard.com/find-a-provider
- Call member services at 1-800-HEALTH-G (1-800-432-5844)
- Use the HealthGuard mobile app
- Provider directory updated monthly

REFERRALS:
- HMO plans require referrals from primary care physician for specialists
- PPO plans do not require referrals
- HealthGuard Basic is an HMO plan
- HealthGuard Plus and Premium are PPO plans

=== CLAIMS ===

HOW TO FILE A CLAIM:
1. In-network providers file claims directly - no action needed from you
2. Out-of-network claims: Submit claim form within 90 days of service
3. Required documentation: Itemized bill, provider information, diagnosis codes
4. Submit online at healthguard.com/claims, by mail, or by fax
5. Claims are typically processed within 15-30 business days

CLAIM STATUS:
- Check status online at healthguard.com/members
- Call member services at 1-800-HEALTH-G
- Use the HealthGuard mobile app
- EOB (Explanation of Benefits) mailed within 30 days of processing

APPEALING A DENIED CLAIM:
- You have 180 days to file an appeal
- Submit in writing with supporting documentation
- Internal review completed within 30 days (standard) or 72 hours (urgent)
- External review available if internal appeal is denied
- Contact member services for appeal form

=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

Q: When does my coverage start?
A: Coverage typically begins the first of the month following enrollment. For open enrollment, coverage starts January 1st. Special enrollment coverage starts the first of the month following the qualifying event.

Q: Can I add dependents to my plan?
A: Yes, you can add dependents during open enrollment or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, adoption, loss of other coverage).

Q: What is a deductible?
A: A deductible is the amount you pay for covered healthcare services before your insurance plan starts to pay. Preventive services are covered before you meet your deductible.

Q: What is an out-of-pocket maximum?
A: The most you have to pay for covered services in a plan year. After you reach this amount, your plan pays 100% for covered services. Premiums don't count toward the out-of-pocket maximum.

Q: What is coinsurance?
A: Your share of costs of a covered healthcare service, calculated as a percentage. For example, if coinsurance is 20%, you pay 20% and the plan pays 80%.

Q: What is a copay?
A: A fixed amount you pay for a covered healthcare service. For example, $25 for a doctor visit.

Q: Do I need a referral to see a specialist?
A: It depends on your plan. HealthGuard Basic (HMO) requires a referral from your primary care physician. HealthGuard Plus and Premium (PPO) do not require referrals.

Q: Is telehealth covered?
A: Yes, all HealthGuard plans cover telehealth visits. Copays range from $0 to $15 depending on your plan.

Q: What if I need emergency care out of state?
A: Emergency care is covered nationwide at in-network rates, regardless of which provider you see. You do not need prior authorization for emergency services.

Q: How do I get a new ID card?
A: Request a new card online at healthguard.com/members, through the mobile app, or by calling 1-800-HEALTH-G. Digital ID cards are available instantly in the app.

Q: What is the difference between HMO and PPO?
A: HMO (Health Maintenance Organization) plans generally require you to choose a primary care physician and get referrals for specialists, but have lower premiums. PPO (Preferred Provider Organization) plans offer more flexibility to see any provider without referrals, but typically have higher premiums.

Q: Are pre-existing conditions covered?
A: Yes, all HealthGuard plans cover pre-existing conditions. No waiting periods or exclusions apply.

Q: What preventive care is covered at 100%?
A: Annual physicals, immunizations, cancer screenings (mammograms, colonoscopies, pap smears), blood pressure screening, cholesterol screening, diabetes screening, depression screening, well-child visits, and prenatal care.
